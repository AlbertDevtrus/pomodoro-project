What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses 25-minute work intervals, called "pomodoros," followed by short breaks. It was developed to enhance concentration and productivity.

Advantages of the Pomodoro Technique

This structured approach increases efficiency by reducing procrastination and mental fatigue. By breaking work into manageable segments, it promotes concentration and improves the quality of time dedicated to each task.
